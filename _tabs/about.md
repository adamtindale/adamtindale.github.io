## About Me
I'm Adam, a network engineer with over a decade of experience in enterprise networking and infrastructure. Day to day I work across data centre and campus networks — design, implementation, and the inevitable troubleshooting.

Outside of work, I run a homelab and a home brewery out of my garage. You'll find posts on both here, along with the odd networking topic.

## Why blog?
Mostly to document things I've figured out or found useful. If it helps someone else along the way, even better.
